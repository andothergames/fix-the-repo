Welcome to the world of dinosaurs! Dinosaurs, the fascinating giants that once roamed our planet, have captured imaginations for generations. These reptiles first appeared during the Triassic period, approximately 230 million years ago, and dominated life on Earth throughout the Mesozoic Era, which lasted until around 66 million years ago. The Mesozoic is often divided into three periods: Triassic, Jurassic, and Cretaceous. Throughout these epochs, dinosaurs evolved into an incredible diversity of species, ranging from the towering, long-necked Brachiosaurus to the nimble, feathered Velociraptor.

Popular Dinosaurs

Tyrannosaurus Rex

The T. rex was one of the most fearsome dinosaurs, known for its powerful bite and large size.

Triceratops

Triceratops was a large herbivore with three horns and a massive frill on its head.

Velociraptor

Velociraptors were small, agile predators that hunted in packs.
